Select your audio delay in milliseconds. Move the dot and use ↓ and ↑ to fine tune. If the extension is enabled, the changes will affect instantly any YouTube videos that are playing.

If you use TuneBlade, set your Port to Manual in TuneBlade Settings -> Remote Control and Apply. Copy the Port value here to allow the extension to communicate with TuneBlade.
